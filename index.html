What 3 Words

Nov 04, 2021
bookmark
link / bookmark / map

https://what3words.com/ I am trying to find the purpose for this site. It maps out any place on the globe with three words. Esentially, it has divided the entire Earth in a grid and given a three word assignment to each square. For example: The Eiffel Tower is at prices.slippery.traps (Which is actually funny considering all the commerce around the landmark.) Other site examples: Eiffel Tower: ///prices.slippery.traps Trevi Fountain: ///received.switch.repeats Wrigly Field: ///flops.

Invent-a-Word

Nov 02, 2021
bookmark
link / bookmark

https://www.degraeve.com/invent-a-word/ I like using this site to come up with creative names. It will combine the beginning or ending of a given word with other matching words in the dictionary. Example: travel + velocity = travelocity
